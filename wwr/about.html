Make Your Mark on the Rapids

Purpose: To connect people with the raw power of nature through safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable whitewater adventures.

Mission: To deliver expertly guided, eco-friendly river excursions that inspire environmental stewardship, build lasting teamwork, and create lifelong memories.

Motto: Chase the rapids. Respect the flow.

History

The Whitewater Rafting Company was founded in 2005 with a passion for sharing the thrill of river adventures. What started as a small group of enthusiasts has grown into a leading provider of whitewater rafting experiences.
